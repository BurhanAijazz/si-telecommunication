## Exam Strategy Tips

1. **Time Management:** 100 questions in 120 minutes = ~72 seconds per question. Don't spend more than 90 seconds on any question.
2. **Negative Marking:** Each wrong answer costs 0.5 marks. Only attempt questions you are reasonably confident about (>60% sure).
3. **Attempt Order:** Start with your strongest section first to build confidence and secure marks.
4. **Part B Priority:** Technical section carries 140/200 marks (70%). Focus maximum energy here.
5. **High-Weight Subjects:** Subjects 7, 8, 9, 10 carry 20 marks each (80 marks total = 40% of exam). Master these first.
6. **Guessing Strategy:** With -0.5 penalty, guessing randomly among 4 options has negative expected value. Only guess when you can eliminate at least 2 options.
7. **Formula Sheet:** Maintain a running formula sheet throughout your preparation for quick last-day revision.
8. **Daily Practice:** Solve at least 10-15 MCQs daily from Day 1.
